Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are automatically placed on the electronic devices used by the user when visiting the website. These downloaded files can store data that can be updated and retrieved by the entity responsible for their installation. These files allow us to know, for statistical purposes, to improve the service and adapt it to your preferences, your behavior when browsing the web. Cookies are only associated with your browser and do not in themselves provide personal data. The cookies used on this website do not contain viruses or other harmful components, and do not in themselves pose a risk to the user's devices.

This Website uses the following cookies:

Types of cookies according to the entity that manages them:

  • Own cookies: These are those established by the website visited by the user. The data collected through these cookies is used for purposes such as the calculation of page views, sessions, and number of users.
  • Third-party cookies: These are those that are sent to the user's terminal equipment from a computer or domain that is not managed by the publisher, but by another entity that processes the data obtained through cookies, or cookies installed from a computer or domain managed by the publisher itself, but the information collected is managed by a third party.

Types of cookies according to their purpose:

  • Technical/necessary cookies: These are those that allow the user to perform the basic functions such as browsing the page and accessing secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies, so their installation is mandatory.
  • Advertising cookies: These are those that, whether processed by this website or by third parties, allow us to manage in the most effective way possible the offer of advertising spaces that are on the website, adapting the content of the advertisement to the content of the requested service or the use you make of our website. To do this, we can analyze your Internet browsing habits and we can show you advertising related to your browsing profile.
  • Behavioral advertising cookies: These are those that allow the management, in the most effective way possible, of the advertising spaces that, where appropriate, the publisher has included on a website, application, or platform from which the requested service is provided. This type of cookie stores information on the behavior of visitors obtained through the continuous observation of their browsing habits, which allows the development of a specific profile to display advertising based on it.
